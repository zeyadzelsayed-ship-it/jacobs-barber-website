The Shop

Built one fade at a time, one chair at a time.

Jacobs started as a single chair and grew into Tadley's go-to barbershop the honest way — by getting the cut right, every time, and remembering your name when you walk back in.

The team now spans seasoned hands and sharp new talent, but the standard doesn't move: precise skin fades, unhurried beard work, and a shop floor where kids, first-timers and thirty-year regulars all get the same care in the chair.

Booking's easy through the app, walk-ins are never turned away when there's room on the board, and the kettle's always on.
